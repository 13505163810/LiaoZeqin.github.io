Welcome to Zeqin Liao's Homepage

I am now pursuing an Ph.D in School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen (Zhongshan) University, under the supervision of Prof. Zibin Zheng (IEEE Fellow). I obtained my master degree from HoHai University under the supervision of Prof. Guangjie Han (IEEE Fellow) in 2021. I am an active authors in terms of publishing at leading software engineering task.

My research interests broadly fall within the areas of vulnerability analysis and software security , with a primary focus on analyzing and improving the security of smart contract. My research generally involves building pratical softwares and tools to detect/address various security threats.

当前我正攻读中山大学软件工程学院博士学位, 师从 郑子彬教授 (IEEE Fellow, 中山大学软件工程学院副院长)。 此前，我于2021年从河海大学 获得了硕士学位 师从韩光洁教授 (IEEE Fellow，河海大学信息科学与工程学院院长)。 我是一名在软件工程会议和期刊上出版文章的活跃的工作者。

我的研究兴趣包括 漏洞分析 和 软件安全 等领域, 主要聚焦于分析和提高智能合约的安全性. 我的研究通常涉及构建实用的软件或工具来检测或解决各种安全威胁。
