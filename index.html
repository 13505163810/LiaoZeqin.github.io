Welcome to Zeqin Liao's Homepage

I am now working as an Research Fellow in College of Computing and Data Science, Nanyang Technological University, under the supervision of Prof. Yang Liu (IEEE Fellow). I obtained my Ph.D degree from Sun Yat-sen (Zhongshan) University under the supervision of Prof. Zibin Zheng (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Yuhong Nan in 2025. I am an active authors in terms of publishing at leading software engineering task.

My research interests broadly fall within the areas of vulnerability analysis and software security , with a primary focus on analyzing and improving the security of smart contract. My research generally involves building pratical softwares and tools to detect/address various security threats.
