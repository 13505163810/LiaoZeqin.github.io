Introduction

I am now an Associate professor in School of Software Engineering, Sun Yat-sen (Zhongshan) University. Previously, I was a Post-doctoral Research Associate at Purdue University, Department of Computer Science, working with Prof. Dongyan Xu. I obtained my PhD from Fudan University under the supervision of Prof. Min Yang in 2018. I am also fortunate to closely work with Prof. XiaoFeng Wang from Indiana University at Bloomington.

My research interests broadly fall within the areas of systems software security and privacy, with a primary focus on analyzing and improving the security and privacy of IoT, mobile and other emerging platforms. My research generally involves building real systems and tools to detect and address various security and privacy threats.

I am always looking for self-motivated students to work with me at Sun Yat-Sen University. Please email me your CV if you are interested in joining my group.
